\## 📖 Preface: A Largely Harmless Introduction



Far out in the uncharted backwaters of the unfashionable end of the Western Spiral Arm of the internet, there lies a small, largely forgotten collection of GitHub repositories.  



In one of these repositories, orbiting at a distance of roughly zero parsecs from your screen, is a document that could have been called \*anything\*.  



It was almost called \*\*A Beginner's Guide to AI Agents\*\*.  

It was briefly called \*\*AI Agents for Dummies\*\*, but legal got involved.  

It was considered, then rejected, as \*\*Please Stop Asking What an Agent Is, I Have a Demo to Give\*\*.  



It is called \*\*The Hitchhiker's Guide to AI Agents\*\*.  



> Not because the guide will help you hitchhike (it won't),  

> but because both the galaxy and the AI agent landscape have something very important in common:  

> \*\*they are both mind-bogglingly vast, mostly incomprehensible, and populated by entities that seem to know exactly what they're doing but are, in fact, making it up as they go.\*\* 🌌🤖
